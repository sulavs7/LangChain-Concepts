On the field where green grass grows,
The summer wind gently blows.
Bats are ready, hearts beat fast,
A moment built to truly last.

The bowler runs with focused eye,
The ball is thrown, it cuts the sky.
The bat swings strong, a mighty sound,
The ball flies high, beyond the ground.

Cheers erupt from every stand,
Joy and hope go hand in hand.
Runs are made with skill and grace,
Victory smiles on every face.

Win or lose, the game is fair,
Spirit of cricket fills the air.
A bond of joy for all to see—
A game of life, of unity.
